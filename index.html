What I Do

As a dedicated yoga instructor specializing in Applied Yoga Anatomy and Muscle Activation, I focus on empowering individuals on their journey towards strength, mobility, and a pain-free life. Through personalized assessments, I evaluate each student's muscular condition to tailor a yoga practice that precisely meets their needs. My approach involves identifying the perfect balance between strength-building and enhancing mobility, ensuring each practice is a step towards overall well-being. I provide specialized muscle activation techniques designed to progressively develop core strength and flexibility, laying the foundation for a sustainable, pain-free yoga practice. My goal is to guide students as they unlock their body's potential, leading them towards a healthier, more balanced life.

Who I Am

As a spiritual aspirant and dedicated meditator, my life is devoted to delving into the depths of consciousness and harnessing the transformative essence of yoga. My explorations have led me across the globe, enriching my life with diverse cultural experiences and deep spiritual practices. I've embraced the ancient wisdom of Kriya Yoga and other profound techniques like Vipassana, Kundalini Kriya Yoga, Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya, Sahaja Yoga, and Sudarshan Kriya, each of which has profoundly reshaped my understanding of life. My thirst for adventure transcends physical boundaries, guiding me through the vast and unexplored realms of mind and spirit. My journey is not just a quest for personal enlightenment; it's a mission to inspire and guide others towards their own paths of self-discovery and holistic wellness.
